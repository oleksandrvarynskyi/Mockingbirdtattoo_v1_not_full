Beforecare

How to prepare before your appointment

Eat a proper meal before your appointment and keep water with you through the day. Please avoid alcohol for at least 24 hours before the session, arrive rested, and wear comfortable clothing that gives easy access to the tattoo or piercing area. Bring reference images, placement ideas and any questions you want to discuss with your artist.

Tattoo Aftercare

Healing instructions

Keep the fresh tattoo clean and follow the wrap instructions given by your artist. Wash gently with clean hands and fragrance-free soap, pat dry with a clean paper towel, and use only a thin layer of recommended aftercare product. While healing, avoid soaking, swimming pools, hot tubs, direct sun and picking or scratching at peeling skin.

Piercing Aftercare

Piercing care guide

Clean the piercing with sterile saline solution and wash your hands before touching the area for any reason. Do not twist or rotate jewelry during healing, because unnecessary movement can irritate the piercing. Keep the area dry, avoid sleeping directly on a fresh piercing when possible, and contact the studio if swelling, irritation or discomfort feels unusual.
