Colby

Hi, I’m Colby.

Originally from Saskatchewan, I took a bit of an unconventional path into tattooing. I started out in HR before moving into hair, but tattooing has always been part of my life in one way or another. I started getting tattooed when I was 17 and spent years around shops, artists, and the culture long before I ever picked up a machine myself. After a bit of a roundabout journey, I finally found my way into apprenticing at Mockingbird (I’m home!).

Along with tattooing, I also help manage parts of the shop, which has made me appreciate how important the overall environment is for both clients and artists. I care a lot about helping create a space that feels safe, welcoming, comfortable and genuinely enjoyable to be in for everyone involved.

I’m open to working in a variety of styles, but naturally gravitate toward simple, clean and bold designs, different forms of ornamental work, and tattoos that are a little odd in the best way. I love collaborating with clients to take an idea and turn it into a tattoo that not only fits their vision, but also works well with their body, flows naturally (or unnaturally if you’re into that) and stands the test of time.

Let’s chat!
