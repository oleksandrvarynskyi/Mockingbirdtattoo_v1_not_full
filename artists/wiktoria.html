Dark-fairy

Wiktoria

Oh, hey you! ❤ I’m Wiktoria, but you can call me Viki. I’ve been tattooing for 3 years now. I’m a Polish tattoo artist based in Calgary, Canada.

I call my style dark-fairy - because I think it’s the best name for what I do, what I feel and how I would describe myself. My style focuses on graphic designs with a mix of illustration art and anime vibes. I also love playing around with micro-realism and blackwork elements. My biggest inspirations are tales and nature—whether it’s the real deal or something straight out of a fantasy world. Expect to see lots of animals, plants, and iconic cartoon characters in my designs. I’m all about fine lines and paying attention to every tiny detail. But don’t worry! If you’re expecting anything else, we could figure something out.

When I’m not tattooing, I’m usually at home chilling with my husband and our two cats. I love getting lost in tv shows or movies (Disney classics are my favorite ones), diving into a good book or exploring the worlds of Nintendo games. Oh, and fun fact—I’m secretly a Pokémon Champion. Yep, I’ve beaten all the Elite Four! ✨

If you’d like to book a tattoo, feel free to message me on Instagram @vixey.tattoo or email me at vixey.kmita@gmail.com.
