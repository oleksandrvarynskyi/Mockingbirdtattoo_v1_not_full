The studio

A calm, artistic space for tattoos, piercing and custom ideas.

A place where passion meets professionalism. Our artists specialize in various styles, creating custom tattoos for you. We prioritize professionalism, safety, and a welcoming atmosphere, ensuring clients feel comfortable and cared for. Whether you're looking for your next piece, a spontaneous walk-in, or want to get tattooed by one of our guest artists, we've got you covered.

Services

Tattoo and piercing services shaped around your idea, placement and style.

From custom tattoo projects to carefully placed piercings, every appointment starts with a clear conversation about your idea, anatomy, comfort and long-term result. Our studio keeps the process calm, personal and detail-focused — from the first reference image to the final aftercare steps.
