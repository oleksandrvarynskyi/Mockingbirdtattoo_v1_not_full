Tattoo work

Custom tattoo projects built around idea, placement and style.

Every tattoo begins with your idea and a conversation about placement, size, flow and long-term wear. Bring reference images, describe the feeling you want, and we will help match the project with the right artist and approach. Whether it is a small fine-line piece, bold blackwork, ornamental detail or a larger custom composition, the goal is the same: a tattoo that feels personal, balanced and made for your body.

Fine line

Delicate details, softer linework and minimal compositions that depend on clean placement and precision.

Blackwork

High contrast pieces, graphic shapes, darker compositions and bold visual weight.

Custom pieces

Original concepts developed from your references, story, placement and preferred artist style.

Piercing

Piercing appointments are available with one dedicated specialist.

Piercing appointments are handled with attention to anatomy, jewelry choice, symmetry and healing. We keep the process straightforward and comfortable, explain placement before anything is done, and provide clear aftercare so your new piercing has the best chance to heal cleanly.
